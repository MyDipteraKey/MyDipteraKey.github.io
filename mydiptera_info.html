My intention is to use this site as a starting point when it comes to Diptera identification. I have tried to gather available documents and images from the Internet (free, with no copyright-violation), and to combine them with my own images into a more up-to-date and easy editable identification key.

Any contribution with keys, information, images will be appreciated.

If anyone find their documents or images on this site and want them removed, please contact me at skrylten(at)gmail(dot)com, and I will remove them ASAP.
