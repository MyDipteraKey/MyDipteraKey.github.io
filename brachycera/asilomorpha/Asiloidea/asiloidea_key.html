Nemestrinoidea

- Empodium bristle-like or not discernible, at most 2 well-developed pads below claws. Veins CuA2 and A1 variable. If empodium (secondarily, in some Empidoidea) pad-like, CuA2 and A1 meeting far from wing margin and not in an acute angle, and only 3 flagellomeres present in the form of a large first one and a two-segmented stylus.
